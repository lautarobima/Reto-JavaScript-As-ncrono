ID. CockTail

Gin, Chocolate liqueur, Amaretto, Chocolate Sauce, Salted Chocolate Add ice, toffee gin, chocolate liqueur and Amaretto to a cocktail shaker and shake vigorously. Pour the chocolate syrup into a saucer and dip the top of a martini glass into the sauce. Grate some of the Willie\'s sea salt chocolate into another saucer and dip the coated glass, so the flakes stick to the sauce, creating a chocolate rim! Pour the contents of the shaker into your chocolatey glass and sprinkle with more grated chocolate - enjoy!
